# Wenpei Li

Personal academic website for Wenpei Li, Research Assistant at the Urban Analytics Lab, National University of Singapore.

Website: https://wenpeimuzi.github.io/

## About

Wenpei Li develops human-centred green space analytics to understand how urban parks and green spaces are described, used, perceived, and assessed. Her work connects landscape architecture with urban data science, using geospatial analysis, machine learning, natural language processing, large language models, and multimodal urban data.

## Research Interests

- Landscape planning
- Urban analytics
- Public health
- Multimodal urban data
- Green space assessment
